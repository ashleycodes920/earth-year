Welcome to Earth Year!

Make Mother Earth Beautiful Again

Earth Year is where volunteers come together to make the earth beautiful and healthy again. We are proud to have such an amazing team and are always looking for more hands-on volunteers!

Whether you are taking it to the streets, your local park or the beach Earth Year is grateful for you to be apart of this movement. Don't hesitate to sign up down below! We will be sending updates on areas being cleaned with the help of all of you sharing your progress. And if anyone knows any places that need to be cleaned please let us know! Let's make mother nature beautiful again!
